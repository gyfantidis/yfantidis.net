Read More About Me

Computer Science has always been a field that I have been passionate about. My interest in this field led me to pursue my studies in Computer Science at the School of Science and Technology of Hellenic Open University (HOU), while also working as a consultant in agriculture and veterinary investments.

After successfully completing the 5-year curriculum, I am proud to have graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. This academic certification allows me to confidently make a career change and move forward in my professional development.

I am excited about the many opportunities that are now available to me with my background in computer science. I am determined to use my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in my chosen career through hard work and dedication.

  • SAMARITAN IN HELLENIC RED CROSS

    Volunteering as a Samaritan for the Hellenic Red Cross has been a deeply rewarding experience for me. I am grateful for the opportunity to give back to my community and make a positive impact on the lives of others. In 2015, I was fortunate to be able to support refugees from Syria in Idomeni, Greece. It was a challenging and emotional time, but I was touched by the resilience and strength of the people I met. I feel proud to have played a small part in helping to make a difference in their lives. Volunteering with the Hellenic Red Cross has taught me the importance of compassion, empathy, and the power of making a positive impact on the world.

  • Traveling

    Traveling is my greatest passion. There is nothing that I enjoy more than discovering new places, immersing myself in different cultures, and making memories that will last a lifetime. Whether I'm exploring a bustling city, relaxing on a sandy beach, or embarking on an adventure through the mountains, I always find a way to make the most of my travels. I love the thrill of planning a trip, the excitement of arriving in a new destination, and the sense of accomplishment that comes with overcoming any challenges that may arise. Traveling allows me to broaden my perspective, learn about the world, and grow as a person. It is an integral part of who I am and brings me joy and fulfillment every single day.

  • Running

    Running is my favorite hobby. It allows me to clear my mind, stay active, and explore my surroundings. Whether I'm pounding the pavement in my neighborhood or hitting the trails in the great outdoors, running helps me to feel energized and accomplished. There is nothing like the feeling of standing at the top of a mountain, taking in the breathtaking views, and feeling a sense of accomplishment. Hiking allows me to connect with nature, get some exercise, and clear my mind. I love the sense of adventure and discovery that comes with exploring new trails and discovering hidden gems along the way. Whether I'm tackling a challenging ascent or enjoying a leisurely stroll, hiking brings me joy and fulfillment every time. Olympos is a special place for me, and I can't wait to return and experience all that it has to offer.

  • Refereeing

    I have been involved in football for the majority of my life, first as a player and then as a referee in national categories for 15 years. My passion for this sport has never wavered, and I am grateful for the opportunities it has given me. As a referee, I have had the privilege of officiating at the highest level and making important decisions that impact the outcome of a game. I take this responsibility very seriously and have worked hard to develop my skills and knowledge of the game. My years of playing experience have given me a unique understanding of the sport and how to apply the rules fairly and consistently. I am proud of my accomplishments as a football referee and am grateful for the opportunity to be a part of this exciting and challenging profession.
